Manufacturer Of Pipe Fittings Core Products

Buttweld Fittings Manufacturer

Fitwel Industries LLP is a well-known brand in Global Metal Industry. A Leading Pipe Fittings Manufacturers in India. A buttweld fitting is a weldable pipe fitting that allows for change of direction of flow, to branch off, reduce pipe size or attach auxiliary equipment. Forged Steel buttweld fittings are manufactured in accordance with ANSI / ASME B16. 9.

Forged Fittings Manufacturers

Fitwel Industries LLP is a well-known brand in Global Metal Industry. A Leading Forged Pipe Fittings Manufacturers in India. Forged fittings are made from forged alloy material. Forging steel is a process that creates very strong fittings. Alloy is heated to molten temperatures and placed in the dies. The heated steel is then machined into the forged steel fittings.

Flanges Manufacturers

Fitwel Industries LLP is a well-known brand in Global Metal Industry. Leading Flanges Manufacturers in India. Flange is a protruded ridge, lip, or rim, either external or internal, that serves to increase strength; for easy attachment/transfer of contact force with another object; or for stabilizing and guiding the movements of a machine or its parts.
